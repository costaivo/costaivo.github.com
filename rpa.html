RPA: Robotic Process Automation

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is a technology that uses software robots or "bots" to automate repetitive, rule-based tasks traditionally done by humans. These tasks often involve interacting with digital systems, such as entering data, processing transactions, or responding to customer service inquiries. RPA can be applied in various industries to increase efficiency, reduce errors, and free up human workers for more complex, value-added activities.
